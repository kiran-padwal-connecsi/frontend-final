  • Option 1 - Direct Search:
    • Go to your Menu and click on “search” and then select “Influencer”. Alternatively, you may use the “Search” button from your Dashboard view and then select “Influencer” to get the same search panel.
    • Now, fill in your search query.
    • For example: Let’s say you are looking for a YouTube Influencer from United States, who creates Gaming content.
    • Now, you select “YouTube” in your Channels Input, then in Categories you choose “Gaming” and for the Country, You choose United States.
    • You can also adjust the range of Followers and click on search.
    • Now, you see all the results with YouTube statistics of the Influencers that match your criteria.
  • Option 2 - Via Campaign:
    • Go to your Menu and click on “Campaign” and then select “All Campaigns”. Now, click on this icon.
    • Now, the system automatically fills in your search parameters based on your campaign’s requirements.
    • All you have to do is to press the “search” button and you will see the list of Influencers that match your campaign requirements.
  • Option 3 - Via Classified Ads:
    • Check out our Classified Ads section here.
  • Option 4 - Influencers' Offers:
    • Go to your Menu and click on Search and then select Offers. Alternatively, you may use the Search button from your Dashboard view and then select Offers to get the same search panel.
    • Now, fill in your search query.
    • For example: Let’s say you are looking for a YouTube Influencer from United States, who creates Gaming content.
    • Now, you select ‘YouTube’ in your Channels Input, then in Categories you choose ‘Gaming’ and for the Country, You choose ‘United States’.
    • Additionally, you may select – Content-Type, Range of Followers & Price Range of the offers.
    • Now, you see all the offers that match with your requirements and you may respond to the offers by direct messaging the Influencers.
